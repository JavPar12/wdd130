Discover the perfect river for your rafting adventure:

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho: Rapids ranging from Class I to II. Ideal for families and swimming enthusiasts.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah: Rated as Class II/III. A challenging experience with stunning landscapes.

Grand Canyon, Arizona: Rapids rated from Class IV to VI. Combines world-class whitewater with breathtaking scenery.

The river levels vary from gentle to challenging rapids. Consider your skill level and preferences when choosing a river.
